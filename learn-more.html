About our Treatments

LVL Lashes

The LVL lash lift is a luxurious treatment that accentuates your natural lashes with volume, length, and lift. It's a low-maintenance, long-lasting alternative to extensions and mascara, taking only 35-50 minutes to achieve a youthful, refreshed look. Our expert lash artists use specially formulated, vegan-friendly, paraben-free products for a healthy and strong lash enhancement. It can last up to 8 weeks with proper aftercare. Get ready to flaunt your lashes and make heads turn!

HD Brows

Experience the ultimate in eyebrow luxury with HD Brows - a bespoke treatment that's custom-designed just for you. Our expertly trained stylists use a unique design formula and custom-blended color to create the perfect shape and color for your brows, leaving you with a look that's sure to elevate your style and leave you feeling glamorously polished. With results that last 4-6 weeks, and a treatment time of 45 minutes, HD Brows is the perfect way to achieve the perfect brows that will last. Perfect whether you're looking to tame bushy brows or regrow overplucked ones.
